Game Description

**Dot Connect Quest** is a fun and strategic puzzle game where you connect matching dots to solve colorful challenges! Connect dots vertically or horizontally, and form squares to clear all dots of the same color in a single move. Each level has a special goal‚Äîclear colored dots, pop stars, make loops, or conquer tiles‚Äîall within limited moves. With 5 exciting chapters and helpful power-ups, there's always something new to explore!

How to Play

  • Connect Matching Dots: Tap and drag to connect dots of the same color, either **vertically or horizontally**. Connected dots will be removed, and new ones will appear from above.
  • Form Loops for Big Blasts: Create a **closed loop (a square or rectangle)** by connecting same-colored dots to clear all dots of that color from the entire board in a single powerful move.
  • Achieve Level Goals: Each level presents a unique goal, such as clearing a specific number of colored dots, popping stars, or conquering tiles, all within a limited number of moves.
  • Strategize & Use Power-ups: Plan your moves carefully to conserve them. Utilize helpful power-ups like **extra moves, bombs, shuffle, and auto-loop** to conquer tricky levels and complete your colorful challenges!
