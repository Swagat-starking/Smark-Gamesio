Game Description

Welcome to **Purrfect Haven: Cat Resort**, a super cute cat hotel simulation management game! Enjoy charming and vivid cartoon cat animations as you step into the shoes of a resort manager. This is the ultimate vacation spot for all meow stars, and your mission is to create the coziest, most luxurious cat hotel imaginable. Serve a variety of adorable feline guests, catering to their every need, and make your establishment the most popular animal tycoon hotel in the world!

How to Play

  • Build Your Cat Hotel: Construct and expand your cat hotel with various rooms like cozy guest rooms, staff lounges, and a restaurant.
  • Serve Adorable Cats: Welcome a variety of meow stars and cater to their every need, ensuring their purr-fect vacation by fulfilling requests such as room cleaning.
  • Manage Your Staff: Hire and manage your staff to keep the hotel running smoothly, ensuring rooms are clean and guests are happy.
  • Become a Tycoon: Earn money to upgrade facilities, expand your hotel, and become the most popular animal tycoon hotel.
