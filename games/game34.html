Game Description

Defend your garden in Green Guardian: Zombie Siege, an action-packed tower defense! Strategically plant unique flora with special abilities to halt relentless zombie invaders. Unlock and upgrade your plants to unleash powerful attacks and forge an unbeatable defense. Face fearsome undead foes, each with distinct strengths. Earn sun to grow your arsenal and hold the line! Can you outsmart the horde and protect your home?

How to Play

  • Collect star: Tap on the falling star resources to earn currency for your plants.
  • Plant Your Defense: Select unique plants from your arsenal and strategically place them on the grid to defend your garden.
  • Stop the Zombies: Prevent the relentless waves of zombies from reaching the left side of the screen.
  • Upgrade & Strategize: Use different plants with special abilities to counter various zombie types and strengthen your defenses!
