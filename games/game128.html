Game Description

Welcome to **Bluff & Duel: Liar's Tavern**, a world of strategy, wit, and mind games! Success here depends entirely on your wits, not just luck. If you enjoy bluffing, guessing, and a little bit of lying, this is the game for you. Choose between intense **Revolver Duels** or challenging **Dog Duels** and outsmart your opponents in this thrilling test of cunning!

How to Play

  • Choose Your Duel: Step into Liar's Tavern and select your preferred game mode: the strategic **Revolver Duel** or the challenging **Dog Duel**.
  • Master the Revolver Duel: In Revolver Duel, each round grants you one shot with a mix of live and blank bullets. You must strategically decide whether to shoot yourself or your opponent.
  • Bluff and Outwit: Success hinges on your wits! Pay attention to the number of live and blank bullets, and use bluffing and guessing to outsmart your opponent's moves.
  • Achieve Victory: Manage your HP and outlast your opponent. The goal is to either defeat them by depleting their health or, in certain modes, keep your gold coins until the gun runs out of bullets.
