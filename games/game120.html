Game Description

**Kitty Stack Dash** is a fun and addictive stack-and-run game! Help a cute kitten build towers of litter to jump over obstacles and reach the finish line. Tap to stack, time your jumps, and avoid crashing into walls! With simple one-tap gameplay, colorful levels, unlockable kitten characters, and endless mode, it's easy to play but hard to master. Stack up, leap over, and dash to victory!

How to Play

  • Tap to Stack: Tap the screen to make your cute kitten stack piles of litter. This builds a tower, allowing you to overcome obstacles.
  • Time Your Jumps: Carefully time your taps to build the right height for your litter tower, ensuring you perfectly jump over obstacles like wooden crates to continue dashing forward.
  • Avoid Crashes: Be precise! Avoid crashing into walls or obstacles. One wrong move can end your run in this fast-paced game.
  • Dash to Victory: Guide your kitten through fun and colorful levels to reach the finish line, or challenge yourself in endless mode for the highest score!
