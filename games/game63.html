Game Description

In **Triple Match Zen: Magic Connect**, immerse yourself in a world where tranquility meets challenge! Collect three identical items so they seamlessly connect and disappear. Our slow, interesting, and effortlessly easy gameplay is designed to help you relax, while a pleasant atmosphere adds a grain of magic to every move. With a vast array of beautiful items and an infinite number of levels, how far can your matching skills take you? Unwind, connect, and discover your zen!

How to Play

  • **Find Identical Items:** Look for groups of three identical items (like the elf characters) on the board.
  • **Connect & Clear:** Tap or click on the three matching items to connect them, making them disappear.
  • **Achieve Combos:** Strategically clear items to create combos and earn higher scores.
  • **Relax & Progress:** Enjoy the pleasant atmosphere as you clear all items and advance through infinite levels.
