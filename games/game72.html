Game Description

Have you ever wanted to be a hardened security officer on the front lines of maintaining order? In **Elite Guard**, an ultra-realistic role-playing simulator, you play a professional security guard! You decide who enters and who is denied. Some guests may try to sneak in prohibited items – it's your job to find them! Perform critical tasks in a variety of challenging scenarios to identify suspicious individuals and uphold peace. Step up and be the elite guard!

How to Play

  • Inspect Individuals: As a professional security guard, carefully inspect every person attempting to enter your secured area.
  • Use Security Tools: Utilize your scanner and other tools to detect prohibited items that guests may try to sneak in, such as weapons.
  • Decide Entry: Based on your findings, tap the green checkmark to allow entry or the red 'X' to deny access to suspicious individuals or those carrying contraband.
  • Uphold Order: Perform critical tasks in a variety of challenging scenarios to identify suspicious individuals and uphold peace on the front lines.
