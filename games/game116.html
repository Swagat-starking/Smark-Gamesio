Game Description

**Gemstone Switch: Twin Puzzle** is a captivating puzzle platformer! Control two unique characters, Repo and Repo, and strategically switch between them to pick up all the scattered gems on each level. Master their individual abilities and navigate challenging environments to collect every gemstone and conquer the puzzle!

How to Play

  • Control Your Duo: Guide your two unique characters through challenging puzzle platformer levels.
  • Switch Strategically: Press **'X'** to instantly switch between your two characters, a crucial move to navigate obstacles and solve puzzles.
  • Collect All Gems: Your mission is to pick up all the scattered gems on each level. Each character can only collect gems of their corresponding color (e.g., the blue character collects blue gems, and the pink character collects pink gems).
  • Conquer Each Level: Master the individual abilities of each character and strategically use their combined strengths to overcome environmental challenges and collect every gemstone to conquer the puzzle!
