Game Description

Experience the fascinating visuals of **Boat Attack: Emerald Race**! As a motorboat driver, challenge opponents in a thrilling championship. Control your speed boat through narrow track turns, beating rivals amidst emerald waters and towering limestone islands. Choose from two boat types and customize their colors, then race to victory in this visually stunning and action-packed boat racing game!

How to Play

  • Select Your Controls: Before you start, choose your preferred control method—**Keyboard or Mobile**—from the main menu.
  • Start Your Race: Hit the "START" button to launch into the thrilling boat racing championship.
  • Control Your Speedboat: As the motorboat driver, control your speed boat through narrow track turns, navigating the challenging courses on emerald waters.
  • Beat Opponents & Win: Outmaneuver your rivals, maintain your position and aim for the fastest lap times to beat all opponents and win the championship.
