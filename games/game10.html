Game Description

Get behind the wheel in Apex Racer: Grand Prix, where you'll command a high-speed Formula 1 car against clever AI competitors! Race on detailed tracks inspired by famous real-world circuits, experiencing thrilling action, responsive controls, and intense competition focused purely on driving skill. Fine-tune your vehicle, master sharp bends, and push your limits to secure your spot on the podium.

How to Play

  • Choose Your Controls: Select your preferred steering method using the control options presented (e.g., on-screen arrows or a virtual D-pad).
  • Race Your Car: Drive your Formula 1 car at high speeds around the circuit.
  • Complete Laps: Navigate the track to complete the specified number of laps (e.g., "LAP 0/1") to finish each race.
  • Compete for Victory: Outmaneuver and race against other cars to secure the top position.
