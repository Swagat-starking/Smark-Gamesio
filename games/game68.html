Game Description

Welcome to **Skyward Tycoon: Airport Empire**, your ultimate airport management experience! You're in charge of a bustling international hub, tasked with building your airport kingdom from scratch. Plan runways, schedule flights, upgrade facilities, hire employees, and keep the entire operation running smoothly to handle an ever-increasing flow of passengers and cargo. Dreamed of ruling the skies? Now's your chance in this exciting and highly addictive management game!

How to Play

  • Build Your Airport Kingdom: Construct and expand various airport facilities, including check-in desks, security areas, and waiting lounges.
  • Manage Passenger Flow: Guide passengers efficiently through your bustling airport, ensuring a smooth journey from arrival to departure.
  • Earn & Upgrade: Collect profits from your airport operations and use them to upgrade existing facilities and unlock new areas for expansion.
  • Hire & Expand: Recruit employees to help run your international hub and continuously expand your airport empire to accommodate more travelers.
