Game Description

**Flag Frenzy: World Quiz** is a fast-paced, fun, and educational mobile game that challenges players to match countries with their correct flags! Designed for simplicity, it offers a single, highly addictive mode to test your geographical knowledge. In each round, you're given a country name (like France or Japan) and must choose the correct flag from four cleverly designed options. Only one is right—test your attention to detail and become a flag master!

How to Play

  • Identify the Country: In each round, a country name will appear at the top of your screen, challenging your geographical knowledge.
  • Choose the Correct Flag: From the four flag options presented below the country name, tap or click on the one that correctly matches the displayed country.
  • Test Your Attention: Be quick and precise! The other three flag options are cleverly designed to test your attention to detail and flag recognition skills.
  • Boost Your Score: Correct answers will boost your score, pushing you to become the ultimate flag master in this fast-paced and addictive quiz!
