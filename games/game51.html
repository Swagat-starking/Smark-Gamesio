Game Description

Critter Clear: 3D Match is a strategic puzzle game that combines block-pushing mechanics with addictive match-3 gameplay. Push colorful animal blocks around the board to create groups of three or more matching animals, making them disappear. Your goal is to clear all blocks from each level using the fewest moves possible! Navigate increasingly challenging levels featuring special mechanics like ice blocks that freeze animals, and one-way arrow tiles that restrict movement.

How to Play

  • Move & Push Blocks: Use Arrow Keys/WASD to move your character and push colorful animal blocks around the board.
  • Match to Clear: Create groups of three or more identical animal blocks to make them disappear.
  • Clear All Blocks: Your goal is to strategically clear every single block from the level to win.
  • Plan Your Moves: Navigate special mechanics like ice blocks and one-way tiles, planning carefully to clear the board in the fewest moves possible.
