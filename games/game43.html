Game Description

Looking for a fun, satisfying, and addictively casual game? Dive into Tile Master: Perfect Pave! This super uptempo tile-laying management game lets you create the most immaculate floors with joyful clicking. Control your character to place each tile with precision, perfectly filling every space. Continuously upgrade your vehicles and tools to boost efficiency and achieve tiling perfection!

How to Play

  • Lay Tiles: Control your vehicle to precisely place tiles and perfectly fill the designated floor areas.
  • Earn Currency: Collect resources or earn currency as you complete sections of the floor.
  • Upgrade Your Vehicle: Spend your earned currency to upgrade your vehicle's "front width," "brick capacity," "moving speed," and "get brick speed" to increase efficiency.
  • Achieve Perfection: Continuously fill floors to 100% completion by efficiently laying tiles and improving your equipment.
