Game Description

Teeth are so important! Want to show a beautiful smile? Dream of having white and healthy teeth? Come to **Baby Panda Dentist Care: Animal Smile** and help! This is a super cute casual doctor simulation game where you play as a dentist. Accept every adorable animal with dental problems, diagnose their issues, and give them the appropriate treatment to restore their sparkling smiles. Get ready to be the best animal dentist ever!

How to Play

  • Examine Animal Patients: Welcome adorable animal patients into your clinic and carefully examine their teeth to identify various dental problems like broken teeth, cavities, or inflammation.
  • Diagnose Dental Issues: Look closely at the condition of each tooth to diagnose what specific treatment is needed for your cute animal patients.
  • Choose Your Tools: Select the appropriate dental tools from your kit to clean, fill, repair, or extract teeth, providing the necessary treatment.
  • Restore Sparkling Smiles: Perform the treatments to restore white and healthy teeth, ensuring every baby animal leaves with a beautiful, happy smile!
