Game Description

Enter the most nerve-wracking stickman battles! In **Stick Hero: Battle Arena**, you, a mighty stickman hero, fight through waves of enemies using incredible battle skills. This action game lets you upgrade your hero and unleash devastating attacks to dominate every fight! Use punches, various weapons, and powerful skills to deliver deadly attacks. Become the ultimate Stick Hero and conquer the arena!

How to Play

  • Become the Stick Hero: Control your mighty stickman hero using **WASD** to move and engage in intense battles against waves of enemies in various arenas.
  • Unleash Devastating Attacks and Block Incomings: Utilize a variety of incredible battle skills and attacks by pressing the **action buttons (e.g., O, P, U, I, L, J, and K)** to deliver deadly punches, weapon strikes or block incoming attacks.
  • Upgrade Your Hero: Earn currency (e.g., 133 gold) to upgrade your hero's **Attack, Health, and Defense** stats, making them stronger and more resilient for future fights.
  • Conquer the Arena: Fight through waves of enemies, unlock new powerful characters like **Cyberman** with unique abilities, and dominate every battle to become the ultimate Stick Hero!
