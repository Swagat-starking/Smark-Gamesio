Game Description

The Death Worm has surfaced! In **Death Worm: Underground Rampage**, you control a colossal underground monster, bursting through the earth to wreak havoc in a thrilling mix of arcade, action, and casual destruction. Choose from different modes, including Level Mode, where you swim underground at high speeds, lock onto prey, and burst through the ground to deliver a fatal strike! Unleash the monster and dominate the surface!

How to Play

  • Control the Death Worm: Take control of a colossal Death Worm and burrow through the earth using **WASD controls** to move underground at high speeds.
  • Wreak Havoc: Burst through the ground to attack vehicles, people, and other prey, causing massive destruction in a thrilling arcade action experience.
  • Upgrade Your Monster: Earn currency to upgrade your worm's **Attack**, **Speed**, and **Defense**, making your monster more powerful for future rampages.
  • Dominate Different Modes: Choose from various modes like **Level Mode**, **Snake Mode**, or **Endless Mode**, each offering unique challenges as you unleash the monster and dominate the surface.
