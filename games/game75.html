Game Description

Get ready for **Fruit Frenzy: Shish Kabob!** ‚Äî a juicy arcade experience where your aim is true! Aim and shoot a skewer to pierce falling fruits of many types. Master the art of the multi-shot, impaling multiple fruits with one shish for explosive combos and massive points. Challenge yourself across two thrilling modes: aim for the highest scores in Classic, or race against the clock in Time Trial. Compete globally for the top spot and become the ultimate fruit shish kabob master!

How to Play

  • Aim and Shoot: Use your cursor to pull the bottom-central sphere, then aim by dragging in the opposite direction. Release the cursor to shoot your projectile and pierce the falling fruits.
  • Target Fruits Only: Aim carefully to hit the juicy fruits of many types. Avoid hitting any bombs, focusing solely on the fruit to maximize your score.
  • Create Combos: Shoot multiple fruits with a single shish for explosive combos and massive points, aiming for the best scores.
  • Compete in Modes: Challenge your skills in Classic and Time Trial modes to become the ultimate fruit shish kabob master. Your progress is tracked, and you have limited lives.
