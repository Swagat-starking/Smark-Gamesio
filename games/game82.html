Game Description

**Soldier Rush: Evolution Run** is a fun platform running game that blends soldier evolution and puzzle elements into one epic adventure! Lead your soldier through exciting levels, attacking and shooting enemies along the way. Move your troops through an increasing number of doors to grow your ranks and increase weapon power. Unlock different stages of evolution to transform your soldiers into an unstoppable force. Get ready for an evolutionary rush!

How to Play

  • Lead Your Soldiers: Guide your group of soldiers through exciting levels, attacking and shooting enemies along the way.
  • Grow Your Ranks: Move your troops through special doors to increase your numbers and grow your ranks, building a formidable force.
  • Unlock Evolution: Increase your weapon power and upgrade your soldiers' "Year" and "DMG" levels to unlock different stages of evolution, transforming them into an unstoppable army.
  • Conquer the Adventure: Combine platform running action with strategic evolution and puzzle elements to conquer every challenge in this epic adventure.
