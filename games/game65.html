Game Description

Want a fun card battle with friends? **UnoBlitz: Global Cards** brings the classic UNO card game to your screen! Challenge opponents from around the world anytime, anywhere in this fast-paced multiplayer card game. Use the cards in your hand wisely and discover the best strategy in every unpredictable situation to dominate the table. Get ready to blitz your way to victory!

How to Play

  • **Match Cards:** Take turns playing a card from your hand that matches either the color or the number of the top card on the central discard pile.
  • **Draw if No Match:** If you don't have a playable card, draw a card from the draw pile until you can play one or choose to pass.
  • **Be Strategic:** Utilize special action cards like Skips, Reverses, and Wild Cards to outwit opponents and gain an advantage.
  • **Go Out First:** Be the first player to empty your hand of all cards to win the round and dominate the table!
