Game Description

Begin a fun battle like never before in Cosmic Conflict: Rainbow Rampage! You're trapped in a mysterious space station, completely overrun by terrifying rainbow monsters. Your urgent task is to escape the station and rescue your trapped friends. Grab your gun to unleash a furious assault, carefully avoiding enemy attacks while unleashing powerful shooting combos against endless waves of monstrous foes. Can you survive the rainbow rampage and save the day?

How to Play

  • Navigate the Station: Guide your character through the space station to find your friends and escape.
  • Shoot Enemies: Use your gun to attack and defeat the rainbow monsters.
  • Dodge Attacks: Avoid incoming enemy attacks to survive.
  • Rescue Friends: Fight through waves of monsters to rescue your trapped friends and escape the station.
