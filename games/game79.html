Game Description

**Atom Fall: Centrifuge Chaos** is a thrilling arcade survival game that will put your **reflexes** and **focus** to the ultimate test! In this minimalist yet intense experience, you control a small glowing ball, the **atom**, trapped inside a constantly spinning centrifuge. This centrifuge is equipped with deadly **rotating blades** that shift in speed and position as the game progresses, making survival increasingly challenging. How long can you last in the face of centrifugal chaos?

How to Play

  • Control the Atom: Take control of the small glowing ball, the atom, trapped inside a constantly spinning centrifuge.
  • Avoid Deadly Blades: Utilize your reflexes and focus to maneuver the atom and avoid the deadly rotating blades that shift in speed and position.
  • Survive the Chaos: The longer you manage to survive within the centrifugal chaos, the higher your score will be.
  • Test Your Reflexes: This thrilling arcade survival game challenges you to maintain concentration and quick reactions in an increasingly intense experience.
