Game Description

**Rebound Aim Challenge** is a strategic shooter that tests your reaction speed and accuracy! Use advanced object flight physics and rebounds to hit multiple targets in each level. With good graphics and addictive gameplay, are you ready to upgrade your aiming skills and conquer every challenge?

How to Play

  • Aim Your Shot: Use your cursor to **click and hold anywhere on the screen** to aim your shot. A dotted line will appear, showing your trajectory.
  • Utilize Rebounds: Plan your shots using advanced object flight physics to make your projectile **rebound off surfaces**. This is crucial for hitting multiple targets at a level.
  • Hit All Targets: The goal of each level is to hit all objects/shapes with a single shot to clear the level.
  • Upgrade Your Accuracy: Master your reaction speed and accuracy across various challenges to conquer every level and become a Rebound Aim Master!
