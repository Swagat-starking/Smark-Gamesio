Game Description

Get ready to construct on a grand scale and command colossal machinery in MegaBuild Sim: Heavy Haul! Immerse yourself in the most authentic construction driving simulation experience available. Designed for enthusiasts who thrive on mechanics, high-powered vehicles, and mastering significant challenges, this game puts you in the driver's seat of impressive construction operations. Prepare to build big and drive even bigger!

How to Play

  • Drive Your Vehicle: Use the 'A' and 'D' buttons to steer, and 'W' for acceleration and 'S' for reverse/brake.
  • Lift & Transport Cargo: Operate your forklift (or other machinery) to pick up heavy objects like containers.
  • Follow the Path: Drive your cargo to the designated drop-off points, indicated by yellow arrows on the ground.
  • Complete Construction Jobs: Successfully transport and place materials to finish each building task!
