Game Description

**Prize Catcher: Factory Frenzy** is a fun and addictive arcade game! Take control of a virtual claw machine to collect a wide variety of adorable toys. Carefully aim and time your claw to grab plushies, action figures, and surprise collectibles. Stock up your inventory, then head to the factory to sell them for shiny coins! Use your earnings to run the machine and unlock new toys in this charming blend of skill and strategy.

How to Play

  • Aim Your Claw: Use the **directional arrows (left, right, up, down)** on the control panel to carefully aim your virtual claw over the adorable toys inside the machine.
  • Time Your Grab: Once you've aimed, time your drop perfectly to grab plushies, action figures, and surprise collectibles with your claw.
  • Sell for Coins: After collecting toys in your inventory, head to the factory (represented by the **truck icon**) to sell them for shiny coins.
  • Unlock New Toys & Upgrade: Use your earned coins to run the machine again and unlock new, unique toys to add to your collection, expanding your arsenal of collectibles.
