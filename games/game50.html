Game Description

Ready to test your driving skills like a pro? Step into Cargo King: Offroad Haul, the ultimate truck transport simulator built for discerning drivers! Control powerful trucks and complete thrilling transport missions across diverse landscapes. Haul valuable cargo like boxes, barrels, and more, delivering them precisely to designated parking locations. Watch out for treacherous, uneven rocky roads and master slow, steady driving to ensure your precious cargo doesn't fall. Become the undisputed king of offroad transport!

How to Play

  • Control Your Truck: Use the on-screen A/D buttons to steer and W/S buttons to accelerate and brake your powerful truck.
  • Transport Cargo: Load and deliver various cargo, such as the barrel shown, to designated locations.
  • Navigate Offroad: Drive carefully over uneven rocky roads and challenging landscapes.
  • Secure Your Load: Drive slowly and steadily to ensure your precious cargo doesn't fall during transport.
