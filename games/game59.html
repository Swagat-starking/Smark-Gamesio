Game Description

Like speed? Love excitement? Then you can't afford to miss this awesome Orb Rush: Sky Tracks! It's a wild, high-speed 3D platform game that will test your reflexes, timing, and racing spirit to the limit. The operation is deceptively simple but packed with challenges, making it super suitable for those who love thrilling runs and arcade-style action. Control a rolling ball, navigate through tricky, narrow platforms, and avoid falling off into the abyss! Get ready for an adrenaline-fueled roll!

How to Play

  • Control Your Orb: Guide your rolling ball through the wild, high-speed 3D sky tracks.
  • Speed Up: Hold the screen (or click) to accelerate your orb and gain a burst of speed.
  • Navigate & Avoid Falling: Carefully steer your ball through tricky, narrow platforms and avoid falling off the track into the abyss.
  • Race to the Finish: Utilize your reflexes and timing to conquer each level and achieve the best rank.
