Game Description

Embark on the epic saga of the Slime! Dive into Slime Genesis: Elemental Match, a captivating game where strategic attribute matching leads to a deeply satisfying clearance experience. Unravel the story of these unique creatures as you combine their elemental powers. If you enjoy innovative puzzle mechanics and rewarding progression, prepare for an evolving adventure.

How to Play

  • Take Your Turn: Engage in strategic turn-based combat with your slime character.
  • Match Attributes: Enhance your slime's abilities like HP, Attack, Dodge, and Defense by spending Gold.
  • Visit the Shop: Use your collected Gold in the "Shop" to purchase powerful attribute upgrades.
  • Clear the Levels: Strategically build your slime's strengths to defeat enemies and advance through stages!
