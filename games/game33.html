Game Description

Prepare for a delightfully absurd and high-stakes adventure in Canine Apocalypse: Agent Shiba! In a wasteland consumed by a ravenous zombie virus, a uniquely skilled Shiba Inu steps forward, shouldering the monumental mission of rescuing humanity. Witness the hilarious clash when traditional doomsday survival themes collide with an undeniably cute pet agent, setting the stage for a passionate, yet utterly unpredictable, breakout battle! Get ready to unleash the dog of war!

How to Play

  • Control Agent Shiba: Tap or swipe on the grid-like map to move your hero.
  • Combat Zombies: Engage and defeat the zombie-like creatures you encounter with your attacks.
  • Use Abilities: Activate power-ups like bombs, HP potions, or magnets to help clear the area.
  • Survive the Time Limit: Eliminate all enemies within the given time to complete your mission!
