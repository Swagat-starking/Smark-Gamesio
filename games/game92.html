Game Description

**WW2 Frontline: Idle Defense** is an idle war strategy game set during World War II! Command machine gunners defending your frontline wall against endless enemy waves. With each wave, enemies grow stronger and faster. Upgrade your tower, boost game speed, activate powerful artillery strikes, and double your income with bonuses. Build an impenetrable defense, hold the line for as long as possible, and dominate the WW2 frontline!

How to Play

  • Defend the Frontline: Command your machine gunners to defend the frontline wall against endless waves of increasingly stronger enemies. The enemy is coming in waves – hold the line at all costs.
  • Upgrade Your Defenses: Invest your strategic gold to strengthen your fire points, increase damage, and boost your rate of fire. Upgrade your tower and wall to become stronger with permanent upgrades after each battle.
  • Activate Powerful Bonuses: Activate powerful artillery strikes, boost game speed (x2 Speed), and double your income (x2 Income) with powerful bonuses to aid your defense.
  • Earn Rewards & Grow: Destroy enemies for military rations and repel waves for strategic gold from headquarters. Complete combat orders to receive special rewards, allowing you to build an impenetrable defense and hold the line for as long as possible.
