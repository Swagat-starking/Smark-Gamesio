Game Description

If you want to challenge yourself or just relax, **Knife Master: Target Challenge** is for you! Hit all knives on your target to clear each stage. Sharpen your skills and test your accuracy in this fast-paced knife-throwing game! Time your throws perfectly to conquer increasingly difficult levels. Unlock and upgrade a variety of unique knives, each with its own style and speed. Use your tomatoes to improve your arsenal, push your high score, and compete on the global leaderboard!

How to Play

  • Throw Your Knives: Tap or click to launch your knife towards the spinning wooden target, testing your accuracy and timing.
  • Hit the Target, Avoid Collisions: Your primary goal is to stick all your knives into the target without hitting any knives already embedded in it.
  • Utilize Golden Knives: Strategically use golden knives; when thrown, they will remove other knives from the target, helping you clear crowded sections and set new high scores.
