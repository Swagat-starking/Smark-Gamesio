Game Description

In Nitro Street Kings: Drift Rush, buckle up and drive cool racing cars to dominate the asphalt! This is the ultimate street racing simulation game for speed enthusiasts. Feel the thrill of extreme drifting, the realistic roar of powerful engines, and the adrenaline of super-fast street racing! In Race Mode, challenge cunning opponents and collect nitro bottles to unleash explosive speed boosts. Show off your elite driving skills, master every corner, and become the undisputed king of the streets!

How to Play

  • Drive Your Racing Car: Use the A/D buttons to steer and W/S pedals to accelerate and brake your cool racing car on city streets.
  • Race Opponents: Compete against other cars in fast-paced races, aiming to outmaneuver them and reach the finish line first.
  • Unleash Nitro & Drift: Collect nitrogen bottles to activate explosive speed boosts (using the 'N' button) and master extreme drifting around corners with the handbrake (using the 'H' button).
  • Master the Streets: Show off your elite driving skills, navigate challenging corners, and become the undisputed king of the streets.
