Game Description

Welcome to **DuDu Farm Friends**, an amazing casual simulator for little farmers! Be the boss of your own fun-filled farm, brimming with fluffy sheep, clucking chickens, and friendly cows. Your job is to take good care of them: feed the animals, clean their pens, and manage your bustling farm world! Get ready for a heartwarming adventure in farming!

How to Play

  • Be the Farm Boss: Take charge of your very own farm, filled with adorable animals like sheep, chickens, and cows.
  • Care for Your Animals: Your main job is to lovingly care for your farm friends! Drag the appropriate props from your inventory (like food or cleaning tools) onto the animals to feed them, clean their pens, and keep them happy and healthy.
  • Expand Your Farm: Explore your bustling farm world. Unlock new areas, grow crops, and manage various aspects of farm life to expand your operations.
  • Enjoy the Adventure: Continue caring for your animals and managing your farm to build the happiest farm possible in this heartwarming simulator!
