Game Description

Dare to bet your life on an exciting roulette wheel showdown? **Shotgun Roulette: Fate's Trigger** is a gun simulator combining strategy, shooting, and survival. Instead of a pistol, a powerful shotgun dictates your fate. Every pull of the trigger is a choice—the winner lives, the loser dies. Engage in a turn-based shooting roulette game where each round is a high-stakes gamble!

How to Play

  • Understand the Rounds: Each turn, you'll be presented with a shotgun loaded with a fixed number of live and blank rounds.
  • Make Your Choice: In this turn-based game, you must strategically decide your target: yourself or the **Demon** (your opponent).
  • Pull the Trigger: Every pull of the trigger is a choice of fate. If you get a live round, the target will be hit, affecting their health. If it's a blank, you survive that shot.
  • Survive and Conquer: Use your wits to analyze the remaining bullets and make the best decision each turn. The winner lives, the loser dies in this high-stakes game of survival!
