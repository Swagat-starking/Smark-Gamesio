Game Description

Dive into the vibrant, high-speed world of Chroma Rush, an exhilarating next-level challenge! Navigate through an array of dynamic obstacles, controlling your color-shifting ball with precision. Your mission: avoid hitting any object that doesn't match your ball's current color. Start at a gentle pace, but be ready for the speed to rapidly intensify as you progress! This version features a finely tuned difficulty curve, offering levels that grow from surprisingly easy to incredibly challenging, promising an even more addictive and fun experience. Get ready for a blast!

How to Play

  • Control Your Ball: Guide your color ball through the challenging obstacle course.
  • Match Your Color: Only touch objects that are the same color as your ball to survive.
  • Avoid Mismatches: Carefully steer clear of any obstacles that are a different color than your ball.
  • Collect Power-ups: Look for '+' symbols on blocks, which may offer bonuses or help you clear the path!
