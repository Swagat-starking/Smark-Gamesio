Game Description

Prepare for Steel Fury: Tank Blitz, an explosive battlefield experience! Command your tank directly into chaotic combat, with one clear goal: attack, destroy, and survive. Unleash your powerful cannon on enemy tanks and strategically use the terrain to evade incoming fire. With simple controls and epic action, it's perfect for all fans of explosive vehicle combat!

How to Play

  • Control Your Tank: Use the left and right arrow buttons at the bottom left to move your tank across the terrain.
  • Fire Your Cannon: Tap the large button with the bullet icon on the bottom right to shoot projectiles at enemy tanks. Watch its meter to manage your shots.
  • Survive Enemy Waves: Destroy incoming enemy tanks and survive through challenging waves of attacks to earn a high score.
