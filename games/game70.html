How to Play

  • Find Matching Tiles: Identify pairs of identical, unblocked tiles (those not covered or surrounded) featuring cute animals and other surprises.
  • Tap to Clear: Tap or click on two identical free tiles to match them and clear them from the board.
  • Clear All Tiles: Strategize to clear all the colorful tiles from the board before the time runs out.
