Game Description

Dive deep into the vibrant world of fruit-matching with **Fruit Cascade: Merge Drop**! When the same fruits collide, they merge into a larger, juicier one. Your mission is to smartly pair identical fruits to create the biggest possible fruits without letting them overflow from the container. As fruits grow, space becomes a premium – managing it effectively is your key challenge. Get ready for a juicy drop adventure!

How to Play

  • Drop Fruits: Strategically drop fruits into the container from the top to begin your juicy merge adventure.
  • Merge & Grow: When identical fruits collide within the container, they will merge into a larger, juicier fruit.
  • Prevent Overflow: Smartly pair and merge fruits to create the biggest possible ones. Manage your space effectively to prevent the container from overflowing.
  • Aim for Big Fruits: Your mission is to evolve smaller fruits into the largest and rarest types, maximizing your score in this captivating merge drop challenge.
