Game Description

**Cosmic Breaker: Star Blast** launches you into an interstellar arcade experience where classic brick-breaking meets cosmic action! Blast through waves of glowing bricks with powerful space paddles and energy balls in a galaxy full of challenges. Navigate asteroid fields, unlock explosive power-ups, and face off against alien brick formations across vibrant star systems. Upgrade your paddle, unleash laser shots, and become the ultimate breaker of the galaxy. Get ready to bounce, blast, and dominate the cosmos!

How to Play

  • **Control Your Paddle:** Move your powerful space paddle horizontally at the bottom of the screen to bounce the energy ball.
  • **Blast Bricks:** Bounce the energy ball off your paddle to blast through waves of glowing bricks and alien formations.
  • **Collect Power-Ups:** Grab falling power-ups and coins to unleash laser shots, gain extra lives, and upgrade your paddle.
  • **Clear the Galaxy:** Destroy all bricks from each level within the time limit to journey through the stars and dominate the cosmos.
