Game Description

Ready to enter a horror school full of scares and mysteries? In **Horror School: Mystery Unleashed**, you'll play the role of a brave detective! Solve layers of mysteries shrouded in suspense and the unknown within the school's tense and exciting scenes. Choose the complexity of the scenario to start your chilling challenge. Can you unravel the dark secrets before the school consumes you?

How to Play

  • Become a Detective: Step into the role of a brave detective and explore the mysterious and tense scenes within the horror school.
  • Spot Anomalies: Carefully observe each scene for any "anomalous" changes such as moving, disappearing, or appearing items, strange appliances, or even ghosts and pretenders.
  • Report Findings: When you identify an anomaly, select the correct type (e.g., Item, Ghost, Pretender) from the provided list and click the "Report" button to confirm your finding.
  • Unravel Mysteries: Successfully identify and report all anomalies to solve the layered mysteries and unravel the dark secrets of the school before the suspense consumes you.
