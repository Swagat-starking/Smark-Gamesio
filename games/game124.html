Game Description

**Reversi 5: Strategic Grid** offers classic Othello with a twist: get 5 in a row to win! Flip your opponent's stones by trapping them between yours. Victory comes from creating a line of 5 consecutive stones or eliminating all opponent pieces. Play against AI with three difficulty levels (Easy, Normal, Hard) or a friend. Features include a modern interface, AI thinking process visualization, and stat tracking. Perfect for quick games or deep strategy sessions!

How to Play

  • Place Your Stones: Click on an empty square on the board to place your stone (either black or white).
  • Flip Opponent's Stones: When you place a stone, any of your opponent's stones that are trapped directly between your new stone and an existing stone of your color (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) will flip to become your color.
  • Get 5 in a Row: The primary way to win is by creating a continuous line of **5 or more** of your own stones, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  • Eliminate Opponent: Alternatively, you can win by eliminating all of your opponent's pieces from the board.
