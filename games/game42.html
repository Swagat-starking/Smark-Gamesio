Game Description

Love driving games? Crave realistic car simulation with a racing edge? Then you absolutely can't miss Horizon Drift: Open Road! This is a truly crazy driving simulation for adventure-loving players. Dive into an expansive open world where you'll get behind the wheel and embark on thrilling challenges. In Free Mode, conquer diverse objectives, earn rewards, and truly unleash your inner road warrior!

How to Play

  • Drive Your Car: Use the on-screen steering wheel (A/D) to steer and the pedals (W/S) to accelerate and brake your vehicle.
  • Obey Traffic Rules: Follow the instructions and traffic rules displayed on screen to navigate the city safely.
  • Explore & Challenge: Drive freely in the open world, complete various challenges, and earn rewards.
  • Manage Your Vehicle: Keep an eye on your car's status, including its damage, fuel, and engine condition, displayed at the top right.
