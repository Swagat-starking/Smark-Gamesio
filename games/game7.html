Game Description

Dive into Happy Taps: Animal Haven, a cheerful clicker adventure! With every tap, you'll earn rewards, collect coins, and unlock a menagerie of adorable creatures. Customize them with quirky hats, explore charming new worlds, and boost your clicking power to progress effortlessly. It's a cozy and rewarding way to play!

How to Play

  • Tap to Earn Coins: Repeatedly tap the screen (or the main character, like the pig) to collect coins.
  • Upgrade Your Income: Use your collected coins to buy upgrades (like the "STRAWBERRY") that increase how many coins you earn per tap and over time.
  • Unlock & Customize: Spend your coins to unlock new adorable animal characters and dress them up with fun outfits using the icons on the left side of the screen.
  • Discover More: Continue playing and upgrading to unlock new features and charming new worlds in the game!
