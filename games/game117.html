Game Description

**Thorn Blast: Precision Puzzle** is a thrilling action-puzzle game where quick thinking and precision are your best allies! Your mission is simple yet addictive ‚Äì blast away colorful thorns by aiming and launching powerful balls. Clear all thorns from each level with limited shots to achieve victory. How to Play

  • Aim Your Shot: Tap and hold to aim the small black ball towards the colorful thorns (fruits) on the screen.
  • Release to Blast: Release your tap to shoot the ball. You'll need to hit the thorns at the right angle to make them pop and clear them from the level.
  • Clear the Level: Your mission is to clear all the thorns from each level to achieve victory, strategically planning your limited shots.
  • Watch for Obstacles: As you progress through stages, watch out for obstacles that will test your timing, strategy, and aiming skills in this thrilling action-puzzle game!
