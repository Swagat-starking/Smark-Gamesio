Game Description

Get ready for pure chaotic fun in Mayhem Mini Racers: Crash Course! Pilot tiny but mighty supercars on wild tracks brimming with outrageous jumps, cunning obstacles, and explosive mayhem. Smash into rivals, expertly drift around tight corners, and ignite epic crashes as you battle for ultimate victory. Fast, furious, and overflowing with adrenaline, this is the epitome of mini racing madness!

How to Play

  • Race to Win: Drive your mini supercar on wild tracks to outpace your rivals.
  • Cause Chaos: Smash into other cars and cause epic crashes to gain an advantage.
  • Master the Track: Navigate jumps and obstacles, utilizing drifts around tight corners.
  • Survive the Mayhem: Protect your car's health and be the last one standing in this furious race!
