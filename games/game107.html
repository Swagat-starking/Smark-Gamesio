Game Description

Fight off waves of Skibidi invaders in **Skibidi Tower Defense: Stickman Hero**! In this casual tower defense puzzle game, take control of the **Stickman Hero**. Use your strategic skills to build powerful towers and stop the Skibidi army. Move your hero wisely to enhance his power and expand his numbers to attack stronger enemies. Defend your base and crush the Skibidi horde!

How to Play

  • Deploy Your Stickman Hero: Take control of the Stickman Hero and strategically move him by **holding and dragging him** to different places to engage the Skibidi invaders.
  • Stop the Skibidi Army: Fight off waves of giant Skibidi monsters by wisely positioning your hero to enhance his power and expand his numbers to attack stronger enemies.
  • Build Powerful Towers: Use your strategic skills and earned currency (e.g., **dollars and diamonds**) to "Buy Castle" and build powerful towers to stop the Skibidi army's advance.
  • Conquer Levels & Challenges: Defend your base and crush the Skibidi horde by progressing through levels and taking on additional challenges to become the ultimate Stickman Hero!
