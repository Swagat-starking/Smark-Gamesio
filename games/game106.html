Game Description

The city is under attack by colossal monsters! In **Giant Hunt: Sniper Strike**, you, an elite sniper, pick up your powerful gun to defeat these crazy creatures and protect humanity's survival. The fate of the city hangs on every bullet. Use your sniper weapon to shoot accurately from a distance, targeting the monsters' Achilles' heel to bring them down. Become the city's last hope!

How to Play

  • Become the Elite Sniper: Pick up your powerful sniper gun and prepare to defend the city from colossal monster invasions from a first-person perspective.
  • Aim for Weak Points: Use your scope to **zoom in** and precisely target the monsters' Achilles' heel or glowing weak points to deal maximum damage and bring them down quickly.
  • Protect the City: Every bullet counts! Protect the buildings and the survival of humanity by eliminating all giant threats before they destroy your city.
  • Unleash Your Arsenal: Utilize your powerful sniper rifle and strategically use other available weapons to unleash devastating strikes and become the city's last hope.
