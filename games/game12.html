Game Description

Step into Pro Excavator Sim, where you'll control a realistic excavator featuring detailed physics for precise movement and manipulation. Tackle construction tasks of varying difficulty, from simple one-story builds to complex multi-story projects. Earn rewards for each completed level, then invest your earnings to upgrade your equipment or make cosmetic improvements to your character.

How to Play

  • Control Your Excavator: Maneuver your excavator around the site, positioning it for tasks.
  • Operate the Crane Arm: Use controls like the mouse wheel (as indicated) to precisely move and manipulate the crane's arm and bucket.
  • Complete Destruction Tasks: Engage with objects and structures in the environment (like the house) to achieve your demolition goals.
  • Monitor Your Vehicle: Keep an eye on your excavator's status bars (health/energy) at the top of the screen.
