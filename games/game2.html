Game Description

Draw Bridge Puzzle - Draw Game is an exciting and challenging brain game designed to test your problem-solving abilities. Your mission in this unique bridge-building adventure is to draw safe paths, allowing the stranded car to navigate through various obstacles and reach its destination. Prepare for a thrilling journey of creative construction and strategic puzzle-solving!

How to Play

  • Draw Your Path: Tap and drag your finger or mouse across the screen to draw a bridge or path over the gap.
  • Guide the Car: Once your path is drawn, the car will automatically attempt to drive across it.
  • Reach the Flag: Successfully get the red car to the red flag on the other side to complete the level.

Restart if Needed: If your bridge breaks or the car gets stuck, use the curved arrow icon in the top right to restart the level and try again!
