Game Description

Dive into AquaFlow Puzzle, a captivating physics-based adventure where every drop matters! Your mission: help Swampy the alligator by guiding essential water to his broken shower. Navigate through intricate, life-like environments, strategically cutting through dirt to direct fresh water, murky liquids, hazardous toxins, swirling steam, and gooey ooze. Each level presents a unique and increasingly complex challenge, demanding clever solutions to ensure Swampy gets his well-deserved bath!

How to Play

  • Create a Path: Swipe or cut through the dirt to create channels for the water to flow.
  • Guide Water to Swampy: Direct the water so it reaches Swampy's shower at the bottom of the screen.
  • Collect the Stars: Try to guide the water through all three stars on each level to maximize your score.
  • Overcome Obstacles: Navigate around barriers and use the environment to solve the puzzle!
