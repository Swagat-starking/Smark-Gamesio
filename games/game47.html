Game Description

Unwind and challenge your mind in Tile Tranquility: Mahjong Pairs! In this calming solitaire game, your goal is to match and clear all the tiles from the board. Sharpen your observation skills to find and select identical, open tiles, strategically removing them. Featuring beautifully designed classic Mahjong symbols, smooth animations, and a soothing background, each level offers a fresh, engaging puzzle challenge. Earn bonus time, increase your score, and master the art of serene tile matching!

How to Play

  • Find Open Pairs: Look for two identical Mahjong tiles that are "open" â€“ meaning no other tiles are directly on top of them or blocking their left or right sides.
  • Match and Clear: Tap or click on the identical open tiles to select and remove them from the board.
  • Clear the Board: Strategically remove all tile pairs to clear the entire board and complete the level.
  • Beat the Clock: Complete each puzzle within the given time limit to earn your best score!
