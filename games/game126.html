Game Description

**Mythic Army Quest** is an action-packed adventure! Summon soldiers to form your powerful army and battle against evil enemies across a mysterious map. Explore new territories, strategize your attacks, and lead your heroes to victory in this thrilling quest. Perfect for players who love adventure and epic battles!

How to Play

  • Form Your Army: Recruit and summon various soldiers to build your powerful army, ready for battle against evil enemies.
  • Explore the Map: Navigate and explore the mysterious map, uncovering new territories and challenges as you progress through the Roman Era and beyond.
  • Strategize & Battle: Deploy your soldiers strategically in battles against enemy forces. Observe enemy health bars and your army's strength to plan your attacks effectively.
  • Upgrade and Advance: Earn currency and upgrade your army, unlocking new abilities and advancing through different eras to enhance your power and conquer tougher challenges.
