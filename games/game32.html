Game Description

Guide a brave pixel hero through mind-bending levels filled with deadly traps, intricate teleporters, and gravity-defying puzzles in Gravity Flip: Pixel Peril! With a simple tap, you can instantly flip the entire world upside-down, transforming your environment and opening new paths. Leap over menacing spinning saws and skillfully navigate treacherous landscapes to collect glittering gems, unlocking thrilling new worlds as you progress. Be warned: one wrong move in this high-stakes adventure, and it's game over!

How to Play

  • Flip Gravity: Tap the up/down arrow button to instantly flip the world upside-down and change your hero's gravity.
  • Navigate Traps: Use strategic gravity flips and precise movements to avoid deadly spikes and other hazards.
  • Collect Gems: Gather the glittering blue gems found in each level to unlock new worlds.
  • Reach the Exit: Guide your hero through the complex, gravity-defying puzzles to reach the level's end!
