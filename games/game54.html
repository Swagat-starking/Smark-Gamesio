Game Description

The battle has begun in Cannon Clash: Tank Fury! Take aim with your powerful cannons and destroy as many enemy tanks as you can before they eliminate you. Battle against cunning foes, collect game-changing power-ups, and blast your way through over 100 levels of amazing, explosive action. Gather coins from fallen enemies and strategically upgrade your war machine with new cannons, devastating bullets, and reinforced armor to dominate the battlefield and become the ultimate leader!

How to Play

  • Aim Your Cannon: Touch and hold the screen to aim your tank's powerful cannon at enemy tanks.
  • Fire Shots: Release your touch to unleash a devastating shot and destroy your opponents.
  • Earn Money: Defeat enemy tanks to earn money from the battlefield.
  • Upgrade Your War Machine: Use your earnings to buy upgrades like new cannons, stronger bullets, and reinforced armor.
