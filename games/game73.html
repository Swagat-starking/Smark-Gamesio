Game Description

**Shadow Dash: Endless Pursuit** is a fast-paced, adrenaline-pumping runner set in a shadowy, mysterious world. Dash through eerie landscapes filled with traps, crumbling paths, and lurking dangers as you guide your character through the darkness. With precise timing and quick reflexes, avoid deadly obstacles and unlock new areas cloaked in secrets. Collect glowing orbs and power-ups to boost your speed and score. The deeper you run, the darker and more thrilling it becomes. Get ready for an unforgettable, haunting running experience!

How to Play

  • Dash Through Darkness: Guide your character through eerie, shadowy landscapes filled with traps and crumbling paths.
  • Precise Jumps: Tap the left side of the screen to jump left, and the right side to jump right, navigating treacherous platforms.
  • Avoid Obstacles: Use quick reflexes and precise timing to avoid deadly traps and lurking dangers in your endless pursuit.
  • Collect & Boost: Collect glowing orbs and power-ups to enhance your speed and achieve higher scores as you run deeper into the darkness.
