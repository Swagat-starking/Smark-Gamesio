Game Description

**Pool Blitz Pro** is a fast-paced billiards game where precision and speed are the keys to victory! Aim, shoot, and sink balls as quickly as possible to rack up high scores and dominate the table. The faster you clear, the better your rank! With realistic physics, smooth controls, and exciting challenges, it offers an immersive pool experience for casual players and pros alike. Do you have what it takes to become the ultimate pool legend?

How to Play

  • **Aim & Shoot:** Use precise aim and control to shoot the cue ball, sinking all the colored balls into the pockets.
  • **Clear the Table Fast:** Speed is key to achieving high scores and a better rank! Aim to sink all balls as quickly as possible.
  • **Score the 8-Ball Last:** After pocketing all other balls, you must sink the 8-ball to win the game.
  • **Avoid Fouls & Time Out:** Prevent sinking the white ball and avoid committing three fouls, as either will result in a loss. Make sure to clear the table before the time runs out!
