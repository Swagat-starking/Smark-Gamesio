Game Description

Enjoy pure chaos, laughter, and exhilarating action in Stumble Dash: Royale Chaos! This multiplayer running royale game throws you into wild races. Master "seesaw mode" by balancing across shifting platforms and rushing to the finish line. Navigate breakneck speeds through spinning columns, trap floors, and cunning obstacles – each level eliminates the slowest players. In "mountain climbing" mode, be the first to conquer the summit and claim victory!

How to Play

  • Move Your Guy: Use the WASD controls to run and navigate the chaotic obstacle courses.
  • Jump & Dive: Press the "Space" button to jump over obstacles and gaps, and use the "F" button for a dive or dash.
  • Overcome Obstacles: Master balancing on seesaws, dodging spinning columns, and avoiding various traps.
  • Race to the Finish: Be among the first to reach the finish line to avoid elimination and advance to the next round.
