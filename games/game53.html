Game Description

Control your pet's thrilling adventure through a world of perilous obstacles in Pet Hop: Spike Dash! Avoid touching the menacing spikes above and below as you guide your adorable animal hero. There are lots of cute little animals to unlock, but you'll need to rack up a certain number of points to get them. Come and experience this addictive challenge where precision and quick reflexes are key to surviving the spike dash!

How to Play

  • Guide Your Pet: Control your adorable pet as it hops and dashes through challenging levels.
  • Avoid Spikes: Carefully time your movements to avoid touching menacing spikes that appear on the ground and from above.
  • Collect Points: Gather stars or other collectibles to rack up points and unlock new cute animals.
  • Survive the Dash: Test your reflexes to survive as long as you can and achieve the highest score!
