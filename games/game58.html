Game Description

In a dystopian future, the world teeters on the brink of total war. It's a time when humanity desperately needs its peacekeeper. Step into the boots of the Steel Sentinel! Fight relentless hordes of terrorists, enhance your powerful war gear, and reclaim peace across a shattered Earth. Unleash devastating firepower and become the beacon of hope in humanity's darkest hour!

How to Play

  • Move Your Peacekeeper: Use 'W' or the Up Arrow key to move up, and 'S' or the Down Arrow key to move down across the battlefield.
  • Engage the Enemy: Click the Left Mouse Button to shoot your weapon and fight relentless hordes of terrorists.
  • Survive the Battle: Strategically move and unleash firepower to avoid enemy attacks and reclaim peace.
  • Enhance Your Gear: Collect resources during battle to upgrade your war machine with enhanced weapons and armor.
